About Me

Hello! I'm Joey Katach, a student at Brooklyn College majoring in Information Systems. My journey in technology and web development has been fueled by a strong passion for creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My goal is to blend visual appeal with functional robustness, ensuring that each project not only looks great but also delivers seamless, effective user experiences.

Throughout my studies and hands-on experience, I've gained a strong foundation in front-end and back-end development, mastering technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Node.js. I’ve also delved into e-commerce development, content management systems, and digital marketing, giving me a well-rounded understanding of how websites can drive business growth.

I believe in a strategic, client-centered approach to web design. By thoroughly understanding the needs and goals of each project, I ensure that my designs align with the brand and provide value to end-users. Outside of academics, I’m constantly exploring the latest trends and innovations in web development, striving to stay at the forefront of industry advancements.

With a commitment to continuous learning and improvement, I look forward to building a career where I can create impactful, scalable web solutions that help businesses succeed and grow.
